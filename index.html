IoU

Intersection of Universes : Forbidden Zone

 

From cybershamanism to hyperstructuralism

IoU is an artist duo and media art project formed by Benjamin Efrati (FR)+ Shu Isaka (JP) in 2018. "Forbidden Zone" is a multi-layered project connecting Polaroid photography, photogrammetry and web art.
