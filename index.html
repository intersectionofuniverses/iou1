IoU

Intersection of Universes : 禁区 (Forbidden Zone)

 

From cybershamanism to hyperstructuralism

IoU is an artist duo and media art project formed by Benjamin Efrati (FR)+ Shu Isaka (JP) in 2018. 「Forbidden Zone」documents the construction of a 3D model collection which will be used in a VR experience and multimedia exhibition at Chongqing's Dimensions Art Center.
