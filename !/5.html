Intersection of Universes : 禁区 (Forbidden Zone)

 

While wandering in Chongqing's Jiu long po district, one can find a spot vaguely reminiscent of Stalker's "Zone". In Andrei Tarkovski's movie, the three protagonists explore an area filled with magic and mystery. Likewise, many areas in Chongqing, situated between huge buildings, are left wild and filled with iconic wasteland. We wondered whether accessing this "zone" was forbidden. It turned out it is a rather touristic place. This is the entrance of our "Zone".
